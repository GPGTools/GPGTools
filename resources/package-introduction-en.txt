Version 2011.01.27

An installer for a complete suite for sending GPG encrypted mails and managing GPG keys using OS X. Including:

* MacGPG 2.0.17 (incl. gpg-pinentry and gpg-agent)
* GPGMail 1.3.2rc2 (for OS X Mail.app)
* GPG Keychain Access 0.8.3b (managing GPG keys)
* GPGServices 1.4 (OS X text service menu for GPG)
* GPGPreferences 0.6 (GPGTools preferences, gpgmail autofix only)
* Enigmail 1.1.2 (for Mozilla Thunderbird)

WARNING: Please close Apple Mail before continuing!

For further details have a look at http://gpgtools.org.
